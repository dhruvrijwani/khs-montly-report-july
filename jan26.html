💡 Campaign Structure & Creative Performance

Throughout January, we tested multiple campaign structures and ad formats across all platforms including collection ads, carousels, videos, Instagram content creatives, and catalog ads. This diversified approach helped drive consistent performance across Meta, Google, and Pinterest platforms. Notably, Catalog - Top Products and Catalog - High CTR campaigns performed exceptionally well on Meta, while Instagram content as creatives showed strong performance from mid-month to end of January.

💭 Consideration Campaigns

Built brand awareness and audience engagement through lifestyle and inspiration-focused content.

  • Testing Objective: Pinterest was introduced as a new performance marketing channel to evaluate its potential for Kalakaari Haath's visual product catalog. The platform's strength in lifestyle discovery and home decor made it a strategic test for audience expansion.
  • Key Insight: Pinterest showed promising initial performance with a 3.24x ROAS, demonstrating audience receptivity to our product catalog. 🚀 Next Steps & Strategic Focus

Creative Innovation & Testing Roadmap

Building on January's learnings, we will expand our creative testing framework with new formats designed to enhance engagement and conversion:

  • UGC Home Styling Videos: Develop user-generated content showcasing multiple complementary products in real home settings, creating aspirational yet authentic storytelling that drives cross-product discovery.
  • Transformational Product Videos: Create immersive content featuring artisans demonstrating product applications (wallpapers, E-walls, etc.), highlighting craftsmanship and installation to build trust and reduce purchase hesitation.
  • Scale Winning Formats: Continue to amplify the creative formats and campaign structures that delivered strong results in January, particularly catalog campaigns and Instagram content creatives, while maintaining testing discipline.
  • Pinterest Optimization: Refine Pinterest campaign strategy based on January learnings, focusing on high-performing pin formats and audience segments to improve ROAS and contribution to overall revenue.
