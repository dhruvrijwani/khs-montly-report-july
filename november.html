🎯 Next Steps & Strategic Focus
• Sustain Performance Momentum: Building on the strong end-of-month performance, we will continue rigorous A/B testing across multiple campaign variations and creative formats to identify and scale winning combinations.
• Optimize High-Value Product Campaigns: We have restructured the Google Ads Pmax campaign by creating dedicated segments for Wallpaper and E-wall categories. This strategic segmentation allows for more precise budget allocation and optimization, given their significantly higher average order values.
• Product-Specific Creative Development: Based on granular insights from GA4 analytics, we will develop and test category-specific creative assets tailored to high-performing collections including Shangri-Lal, Firdaus, and Serendipity. This targeted approach will improve ad relevance and conversion rates.
• Expand Catalog Campaign Strategy: We will maintain and expand our successful catalog campaigns, integrating both Business-as-Usual (BAU) and seasonal Winter collections. This dual approach ensures consistent performance while capitalizing on seasonal demand through Meta's dynamic product advertising.
