Key Insights & Takeaways 💡

Meta Insights

  • Top Performers Scaled for ROI: The NRI Expats campaign achieved the highest ROAS of 19.1, followed by Retargeting and Wallpaper as the second-highest. Both were scaled after monitoring initial sales performance, driving improved ROI through targeted expansion.
  • Ad Optimization for Revenue Focus: Paused underperforming ads across non-sale and sale campaigns to maximize overall revenue and allocate resources efficiently.
  • Campaign Scaling Strategy: Scaled up the RT and Wallpaper campaigns due to good performance, and scaled down the Fabrics and Decals campaigns because of low ROI. The budget was reallocated to better-performing categories like Wallpaper and Wall Art.

Google Insights

  • P-Max Campaign Performance: Delivered a strong ROAS of 7.42, followed by brands search at 18+ ROAS. Both were scaled after initial sales period based on solid performance, with further expansion into non-sale periods as results remained stable, optimizing long-term efficiency.

Sale Period Analysis

  • Sale Period Outperformance: Sale period achieved 13.1x ROAS compared to 7.8x during non-sale period, demonstrating 68% higher efficiency.
  • Volume Growth: Sale period drove 315 orders vs 257 in non-sale period, representing 23% higher order volume.
  • Revenue Impact: Sale campaigns generated ₹47,85,180 in revenue, more than double the non-sale revenue of ₹21,28,822.
