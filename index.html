What Worked Exceptionally Well
  • Google Ads Dominance: 16.8x ROAS with highly efficient cost per order

Platform-Specific Key Insights

📱 Meta Performance Insights

  • Top Performers Scaled for ROI: The NRI Expats campaign achieved the highest ROAS of 18.12, followed by Wall Art as the second-highest. Both were scaled after monitoring initial sales performance, driving improved ROI through targeted expansion.
  • Ad Optimization for Revenue Focus: Paused underperforming ads across non-sale and sale campaigns to maximize overall revenue and allocate resources efficiently.
  • Creative Relaunch Boosted Results: The RT campaign underperformed initially despite 2-3 days of optimization; relaunching with 5 top-performing creatives from the Sale TOF campaign led to notable ROAS improvements and better overall performance.

🔍 Google Ads Performance Insights

  • P-max Campaign Excellence: Delivered a strong ROAS of 9.41; scaled after initial sales period based on solid performance, with further expansion into non-sale periods as results remained stable, optimizing long-term efficiency.
