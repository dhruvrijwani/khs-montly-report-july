💡 Campaign Structure & Creative Performance

Throughout February, we tested multiple campaign structures and ad formats across all platforms including collection ads, carousels, videos, Instagram content creatives, and catalog ads. This diversified approach helped drive consistent performance across Meta, Google, and Pinterest platforms. On Meta, Catalog - Best Sellers and Catalog - Ewall campaigns performed exceptionally well, while Retargeting campaigns and Interest-based audience campaigns were our top performers throughout the month, delivering strong results consistently.

🚀 Next Steps & Strategic Focus

Creative Innovation & Testing Roadmap

Building on February's performance recovery post-migration, we will expand our creative testing with formats designed to build deeper trust and showcase product value:

  • UGC Home Styling Videos: Develop authentic user-generated content featuring customer reviews, testimonials, and creator partnership videos. This approach builds credibility through real customer experiences and third-party validation, making our products more relatable and trustworthy.
  • Transformational Videos: Create compelling before-and-after videos and carousels that showcase products in application. These visuals will demonstrate the tangible impact of our products, helping customers visualize the transformation they can achieve in their own spaces.
  • Founder-Led Videos: Introduce storytelling content featuring the founder's perspective on the products—highlighting best sellers, explaining what makes them special, and sharing how these artisan pieces add value to customers' lives. This personal touch humanizes the brand and builds emotional connection.
  • Scale Proven Performers: Continue scaling campaigns and creative formats that demonstrated strong results in February, particularly retargeting campaigns, interest-based audiences on Meta, and Brand Search + PMax on Google, while maintaining testing discipline across platforms.
